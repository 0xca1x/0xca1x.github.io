Approach

This is a working site, not a polished portfolio.

I want the material here to stay useful: clear enough to revisit, specific enough to reproduce, and organized well enough to build on later.

If something is published here, it should be worth coming back to.

Principles

  • Write clearly enough for future me to trust and reuse the note.
  • Keep assumptions, environment, and evidence close to the work.
  • Prefer systems that can be inspected all the way down.
